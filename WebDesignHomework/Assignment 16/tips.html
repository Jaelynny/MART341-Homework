Getting Started

Here are some tips to get you started in Minecraft:

  • Gather Resources: Start by gathering wood, stone, and other basic materials.
  • Build a Shelter: Protect yourself from monsters by building a simple shelter.
  • Craft Tools: Make tools like pickaxes and swords to defend yourself and mine more effectively.
